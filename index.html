Joydeep Mitra is a Ph.D. candidate at Kansas State University, U.S.A. He is interested in exploring ways to build secure and robust software systems. He is currently focussed on Android application (app) security. In this context, his work involves developing benchmarks and measures to empirically evaluate solutions related to Android app security along with developing design methodologies that aid in building secure Android apps.

Apart from his primary interest in secure software engineering, he is also interested in developing tools, techniques, and algorithms that facilitate the use and analysis of data. Consequently, he has dabbled with Cheminformatics which deals with use of computers and informational techniques to solve problems related to Chemistry, specifically Crystallography.
